Data Compression With Deep Probabilistic Models

Course by Prof. Robert Bamler at University of Tuebingen.

At a Glance

  • Mondays 16:15-17:45 and Tuesdays 12:15-13:45 on zoom.
  • First lecture: Monday, 19 April; after that, lectures will be on Tuesdays, see detailed tentative schedule below.
  • 6 ECTS with grade based on group project (you may skip the group project if you don't need the ECTS).
  • To encourage interactivity, neither the lectures nor the tutorials will be recorded. However, I'll provide supplementary video material that repeats important concepts discussed in the lectures on the YouTube channel “Tübingen Machine Learning”.
